5 Web Development Trends in 2025

Published on Jan 10, 2025 | By DNK Dev

The world of web development is constantly evolving. In 2025, here are 5 trends that will dominate:

1. AI-Powered Websites

From personalized shopping to smart chatbots, AI is becoming the default.

2. WebAssembly Growth

Near-native performance in the browser with WebAssembly is revolutionizing apps.

3. Jamstack Expansion

Headless CMS and static-first development are making sites faster and more secure.

4. 3D & Immersive Web

WebGL and WebXR are bringing immersive experiences directly to browsers.

5. Enhanced Accessibility

More focus on inclusivity and WCAG compliance in every project.
